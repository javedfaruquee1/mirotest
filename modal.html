Detailed Description of the Topics:

Getting Started with Miro – Webinar

1st of July 14:00 – 15:00 (CET)

A Miro 101 style course, walking through how to get started with Miro including an overview and introduction to our use cases.

Users will learn how to:

  • Navigate Miro seamlessly
  • Create effective, engaging content for your use case
  • Collaborate and connect with your colleagues inside a Miro board
  • Run brainstorms and vote on your favorite ideas
  • Quickly map flows and diagrams
  • Integrate Miro with the tools and platforms you use every day

Hybrid Meetings in Miro – Webinar

1st of July 15:30 – 16:30 (CET)

In this session we walk you through how to use Miro for inclusive, engaging, and productive hybrid meeting experiences that bridge the room and the screen.

Whether you're planning your next kickoff, planning session, pitch, or retro, we're here to help.

Users will learn how to:

  • Leverage features like reactions and stickies to keep engagement and collaboration high in your next meeting
  • Share your board with the right people at the right time to solicit input, feedback, and host instant collaboration moments
  • Design your board for inclusive hybrid experiences, regardless of where your participants are located
  • Use your Miro board for pre-read and action items, to make the most of your precious time

Hosting a Successful Brainstorm in Miro – Webinar

16th of September 14:00 – 15:00 (CET)

In this session we walk you through how to create and facilitate engaging and productive brainstorming sessions. We'll demonstrate in-the-moment techniques that you'll be able to apply right away with your own team.

Users will learn how to:

  • Leverage best practices for creating a welcoming, productive brainstorm
  • Empower your team or group to generate and sort through ideas using Miro tools
  • Use standard and custom templates to encourage great work in your next session

Technical Diagramming in Miro – Webinar

11th of November 14:00 – 15:00 (CET)

In this session we'll focus your attention on the most critical features and skills in Miro to empower you. We'll cover new Smart Diagramming features to customize your shape library and make creation more seamless.

Users will learn how to:

  • Use Miro's Smart Diagramming tools and customize them for your use case
  • Leverage Miro as a one-stop tool to support your entire mapping and diagramming workflow - from ideation to delivery
  • Use features that control how stakeholders interact with your map or diagram
